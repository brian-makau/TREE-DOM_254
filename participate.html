You can also make an impact in the followin ways:

Volunteer

We have a volunteering program, where registered members take part in tree planting activities. Volunteers also take part in online campaigns that include protecting our trees. Each volunteer gets an e-certificate to verify membership.

Donate

Your generous contributions make a great impact to our movement, from buying seedlings to facilitating tree planting activities. Once you donate, a tree is planted in your honour. All these efforts are towards mitigating the negative effects of climate change.

Fun Facts About Trees

  • Trees reduce the effects of climate change.
  • Tree rings can predict climate change.
  • Trees block noise by reducing sound waves.
  • Trees improve water quality.
  • For all organisms on Earth, trees live the longest.
  • Trees increase the bird biodiversity of an area.
  • There are more than 60,000 known tree species on Earth.
